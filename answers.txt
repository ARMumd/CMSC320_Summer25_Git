Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: git merge allows for the combining of all changes from one branch to another, while rebasing moves your branches commits onto another branch, reapplying your changes on top of the other branch. Rebasing has the benefit of creating a more linear history, while merging preserves more context and is less destructive.
